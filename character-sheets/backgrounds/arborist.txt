#### Arborist

You've gained extensive knowledge of the care, nutrition, growth, planting, pruning, and function of trees.  You are also aware of what kinds of creatures live in trees and of what trees which creatures prefer.

Skill Proficiencies: Nature, Survival

Tool Proficiencies: Arborist's tools

Languages: One of your choice

Equipment: Arborist's tools (pruning saw, 2-person felling saw, climbing rope, climbing harness, grappling hook), a set of traveler’s clothes, and a belt pouch containing 10 gp.

Feature: *Tree Expert*
Add your proficiency bonus, a second time if appropriate, to all checks involving trees.
